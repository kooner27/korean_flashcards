noun 정도 = about, approximately, around. degree, amount. formal or informal so bit less common. 

제가 5개 정도 가져갈게요 = i'll bring approximately 5.

이 정도면 충분해 = this much if, enough/sufficient. this much is enough.

noun + 약 = both formal and informal. sounds a bit fancy.
